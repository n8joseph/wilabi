WILABI

Creative Brief for Cover Photo

BIO
Vocalist Amy Toig and multi-instrumentalist Nathan Joseph are WILABI, a Brooklyn-based duo who make seductive, guitar-drenched synthpop.

The pair — who lived next door to each other for a year, unaware of each other’s existences — met when Toig responded to an ad for an open room in Joseph’s apartment.

Toig, a San Francisco native, developed her sound within the prolific Wesleyan University music scene, birthplace of MGMT and Santigold. She clicked instantly with Joseph, who had performed throughout the Northeast for over a decade and shared bills with artists like Janelle Monáe and Dr. Dog.

CREATIVE NEEDS
WILABI is seeking a portrait-style photo for the cover of their debut single. Aside from these broad parameters, the group is looking for creative direction from a talented photographer.
